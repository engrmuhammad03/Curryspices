Who Are We?

NAKHAT Spices is a premium Nigerian spice brand founded by Aminah Hadi Abubakar with a mission to bring authentic Northern Nigerian flavors to kitchens across the nation.

Born from childhood memories of vibrant local markets in Sokoto, we blend tradition with modern quality standards. We are a team of passionate food lovers dedicated to preserving Nigeria's rich culinary heritage through carefully crafted spice blends that tell a story of culture, family, and excellence.

We source the finest natural ingredients and combine them using traditional recipes with a modern twist. Each NAKHAT spice blend is meticulously formulated to deliver consistent, exceptional flavor in every dish.
